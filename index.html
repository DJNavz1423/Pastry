Welcome to Julie's Bakery

We are pastry lovers as you are!

Julie’s Bakery is a family business. Our founder, Julie Heisenberg, is a true fan of cooking and baking. Over the past few years, our team has grown, but each of us shares a heartfelt love for exceptional desserts. We use only natural ingredients, respect traditional recipes, and always strive to add something of our own—new, original, and incredibly tasty.

Every pastry made at Julie’s Bakery is a fresh, stylish, and delicious creation, but most importantly, it is your pastry—crafted especially for you, in your favorite colors, with carefully selected fillings and flavors. Treat yourself to a little holiday of taste!

A delicious pastry is not just a pleasant ending to a holiday evening. A pastry is the true star of the table, and if it fails, it will be remembered for a long time. Conversely, a beautiful and delicious pastry becomes a memorable highlight of your celebration. I have always wanted to create pastry that resonates in the hearts of all sweets lovers. Now I enjoy watching my assistants at Julie’s Bakery care for the pastry-making process with the same love and awe. We love what we do, and you love to taste it!

Julie Heisenberg

Founder
